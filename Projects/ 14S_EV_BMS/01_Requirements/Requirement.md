# 14S EV BMS Design Requirements

## Battery Specifications

* Battery Chemistry: Lithium-Ion
* Cell Configuration: 14 Series
* Nominal Cell Voltage: 3.6V
* Nominal Pack Voltage: 50.4V
* Maximum Pack Voltage: 58.8V

## Electrical Requirements

* Continuous Current: 60A
* Short Circuit Detection: Required
* Over Current Protection: Required
* Over Voltage Protection: Required
* Under Voltage Protection: Required

## Functional Requirements

* Cell Voltage Monitoring
* Cell Balancing
* Pack Current Measurement
* Temperature Monitoring
* CAN Communication
* Fault Reporting

## System Requirements

* TI Analog Front End
* Low Cost 32-bit MCU
* High Side Charge/Discharge Control

## Design Goals

* Low Cost
* High Reliability
* Automotive Oriented Architecture
* Easy Firmware Integration
